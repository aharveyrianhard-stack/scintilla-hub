рҹ”Қ The cliff is the point: counts climb into the near years (40 analysts on FY28 revenue), collapse going out (7 on FY31 EPS), and hit ZERO at FY32 вҖ” the blank columns are real, not decoration. FY22вҖ“FY25 are historical consensus snapshots (what the Street expected then вҖ” beat/miss checks come from comparing them to actuals). FMP publishes counts for revenue and EPS only; middle lines ride between those two. Quarterly (period=quarter): same six lines, ~10 forward quarters, 11вҖ“12 analysts each.
рҹ“Ҡ Line-item depth strategy (per Alan): use the furthest-down line the estimates support, fall back up the statement as coverage thins. For NVDA all five lines exist every year, but the CONFIDENCE thins вҖ” EPS analysts drop 30вҶ’7 by FY2031 while revenue keeps 16. A consensus-based FCF proxy = EBITDA вҲ’ capex вҲ’ cash taxes; capex has no consensus line here, so it gets bridged from historicals (that's the "gap fill from us"). Quarterly estimates exist for near periods; annual carries the long horizon. Agreed base methodology: take the consensus TOP LINE (revenue вҖ” the most analysts, the longest horizon) and CALCULATE the bottom line ourselves via margins вҖ” using deeper consensus lines (EBITDA, net income) as cross-checks where coverage is thick enough. Paid estimate feeds (Zacks/Estimize, low hundreds/mo) logged for later вҖ” not now.
